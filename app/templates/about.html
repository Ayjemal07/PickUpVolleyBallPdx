About Us

This group was founded in spring 2021 on the heels of COVID-19 with the goal of providing a welcoming community when people needed it.

The original inclusion statement has remained the same through the years: “Everyone is welcome at these events. The ONLY exception are those opposed to playing with people of all race, religion, gender, orientation, background, age, and skill level.”

The culture of inclusivity is one of the things that sets this group apart. You can expect to be welcomed and supported by your fellow players.

Check out our events page to get started!
